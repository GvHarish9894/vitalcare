# Product Specification

## Target Users
- Critical patients
- Family caregivers
- Doctors (future)

## Core Features
- Authentication
- Dashboard
- Record Vitals
- History
- Analytics
- Offline Sync
- Settings

## Vital Fields
- Date (Auto, non-editable)
- Time (Editable)
- SpO₂
- Heart Rate
- Blood Pressure (Systolic/Diastolic)
- Remarks

## Business Rules
- Multiple records allowed per day.
- Only today's records can be edited/deleted.
- Past records are read-only.
