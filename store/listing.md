## Full description

VitalCare is a simple, private vitals record book — made to be easy enough
for your grandparents.

RECORD IN SECONDS
Log oxygen saturation (SpO₂), heart rate, and blood pressure with big,
clear inputs and instant validation. Add a note when it matters.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
No account. No sign-up. Every reading is stored only on your device.
The app works completely offline — forever.

SEE THE TREND
Daily, weekly, and monthly charts with min / average / max for every vital,
plus a history you can search and filter.

TAKE IT WITH YOU
Export everything to a CSV spreadsheet anytime, or connect your own
Google Drive for an optional private backup. Restoring merges — it never
deletes a reading.

DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE
Large touch targets, huge legible numbers, light and dark themes, and
out-of-range readings flagged clearly with color and icons.

VitalCare is open source: https://github.com/GvHarish9894/vitalcare

Note: VitalCare is a personal record book, not a medical device. It does not
provide diagnoses or medical advice.

## Privacy questionnaire notes

- All health data stored locally; no data collected by the developer.
- Optional Google Drive backup goes to the user's own account (drive.file).
- Telemetry: currently disabled (no-op); if enabled, anonymous usage/crash
  only, PHI-free, with an in-app opt-out (Settings → Privacy).
