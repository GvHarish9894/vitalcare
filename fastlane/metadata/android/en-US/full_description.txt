VitalCare is a local-first health tracker for recording your vital signs and
fluid balance in seconds — and keeping every reading private, on your own device.

No account. No sign-up. No backend. Your health data never leaves your phone
unless you choose to export it or back it up to your own Google Drive.

WHAT YOU CAN TRACK
• SpO₂ (blood oxygen)
• Heart rate
• Blood pressure (systolic & diastolic)
• Fluid balance — water intake and urine output, with daily goals

FEATURES
• Fast recording with sensible validation ranges
• Dashboard with today's summary and your latest reading
• History with grouping, filters, and search
• Trends and analytics over time
• CSV export you can save or share anywhere
• Optional Google Drive backup to YOUR own account (least-privilege access —
  the app only ever sees the backup files it created)
• Light and dark themes; mL/oz units
• Optional reminders to take a reading

PRIVACY FIRST
• Everything is stored on your device, protected by the OS sandbox and device
  encryption.
• We operate no server for your data — there is nothing for us to see.
• Optional, anonymous, health-data-free diagnostics can be turned off in
  Settings.
• Full privacy policy:
  https://github.com/GvHarish9894/vitalcare/blob/master/PRIVACY.md

OPEN SOURCE
VitalCare is open source. Review the code or contribute at:
https://github.com/GvHarish9894/vitalcare

NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE
VitalCare is a personal record-keeping tool. It is not a medical device and
does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified
healthcare professional about your health.
